Cookie Policy.

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This policy explains how cookies and similar technologies are used on finelinesatelier.com.

The short version: we don't use advertising or tracking cookies. We don't profile you, and we don't sell your data. The only cookies that may be set are essential ones our hosting provider uses to keep the site secure.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files a website stores on your device. They are commonly used to remember preferences, keep you signed in, measure traffic, or track you across sites for advertising.

What we use

Our website is a static site. We do not set cookies to track your behavior, we do not run advertising pixels, and we do not use analytics tools that build a profile of you.

The following third parties are involved in delivering the site, and may process limited technical data:

  • Cloudflare hosts the site and protects it from bots and abuse. Cloudflare may set essential security cookies (such as __cf_bm or cf_clearance) to distinguish real visitors from automated traffic. These are strictly necessary for the site to work securely and are not used for advertising.
  • Google Fonts serves the typefaces used on this site. Your browser requests the font files directly from Google, which means Google may receive your IP address. Google Fonts does not set cookies for this purpose.
  • Web3Forms processes your booking request when you submit the form. It is only involved at the moment you submit, and it is not used to track your browsing.

What we don't use

  • No advertising or retargeting cookies.
  • No social media tracking pixels.
  • No cross-site tracking.
  • No sale or sharing of personal information with advertisers.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Every major browser lets you block cookies, delete existing ones, or be notified when one is set. Blocking essential security cookies may cause parts of the site to work incorrectly.

Instructions are available in the settings or help section of your browser, including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.

Changes to this policy

If we add analytics or any other technology that sets non-essential cookies, we will update this page first and, where required, ask for your consent before setting them.

Contact us

Questions about cookies or your privacy? Email aleena@finelinesatelier.com, or read our Privacy Policy.
