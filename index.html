GOOD TO KNOW

Questions.

How long does it last?+

Nanobrows and lip blushing are semi-permanent and typically last one to three years, with a recommended annual touch-up. Fine line tattoos are permanent. Healing, skin type, and sun exposure all affect longevity.

How much does it cost?+

Each service is priced individually, and brow and lip work includes a perfecting touch-up. You'll get a clear quote after your consult, before anything is booked.

Is it appointment-only?+

Yes. We keep the studio calm and unhurried, so every session is by appointment. Request a booking below and we'll find a time.

What about aftercare?+

You'll leave with a printed guide and a direct message line for the full two-week heal. Keep it clean, moisturised, and out of the sun.
